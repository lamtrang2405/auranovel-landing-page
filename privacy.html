When you create an account or access certain features, we may collect:

2.2 AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED INFORMATION

We automatically collect information about your device and interaction with AuraNovel, including IP address, device model, app version, usage times, and crash logs.

2.3 LOCATION INFORMATION

AuraNovel does not collect precise real-time location information. We only infer general region from IP address.

We collect info on features accessed, playback behavior, preferences, and subscription status to improve the user experience.

3.1 SERVICE DELIVERY AND IMPROVEMENT

We use your info to operate the App, provide playback, personalize recommendations, troubleshoot issues, and manage subscriptions.

3.2 COMMUNICATION

We may use your contact info for service announcements, updates, support, and promotional messages (which you can opt out of).

3.3 ANALYTICS AND LEGAL

We use data to analyze usage trends and comply with legal obligations, enforce terms, and ensure user safety.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

4.2 OTHER SCENARIOS

We may share aggregated/anonymized data for analytics. We may disclose info if required by law, to protect rights, or during a business transfer (merger/acquisition).
